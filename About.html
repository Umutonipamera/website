She has started her schools from (2003-2009) Primary school,(2010-2012) Ordinary level,(2013-2015) Advanced level She has high school degree in Mathematics,Economics and Geography. After finishing her high school she fund a job and she worked hard so that her goals can be achieved Even if it was not easy for her but she tried to save some money and sometimes she used to sacrifice many things for the sake of saving because she wanted to pay university for herself. Anyways everything come for a reason. After some months without working and studying she heard about a program that give opportunites to young women in East Africa countries, it is a one year program that provides tech skills to young women with full scholarship to help them to prepare for their better future.
