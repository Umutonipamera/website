Patricia and Abel's marriage


"Abel wanted a traditional marriage with a traditional wife. For a long time I wondered why he ever married a woman like my mom in the first place, as she was the opposite of that in every way. If he wanted a woman to bow to him, there were plenty of girls back in Tzaneen being raised solely for that purpose. The way my mother always explained it, the traditional man wants a woman to be subservient, but he never falls in love with subservient women. He’s attracted to independent women. “He’s like an exotic bird collector,” she said. “He only wants a woman who is free because his dream is to put her in a cage." Abel wanted a traditional woman because in his culture "Tsonga culture" was extremely patriachad where women from his culture must bow when they great a man and men and women have limited social interaction in that men where the one who used to work for the house and women assigned to stay home with kids,cooking food and take care of all work of the house but finally Abel didn't marry a traditional woman due to he fallen in love with Patricia and end up got married with her even if she was not a kind of traditional woman , the only reason behind their marriage was that they loved eachother.
